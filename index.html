One Sunday a month, Module opens for something else. A techno night that folds in ink and kink — built on one principle: consent is everything. This is not a swingers' club. It's a room where music, art and desire share the same floor, on your terms.

Techno on the floor.

Ink in the corner.

Kink in the room.

Bodies that say yes — out loud.

Module leads the night; Ink & Art brings the ink. Electronic music, tattoo art and kink in one room — sex-positive, radically LGBTQ+-friendly, and built on consent from the door to daybreak. If you're here for that, you're home. If you're not, this isn't your night — and that's completely okay.

01

Is it you?

This is for you if you

  • come for the music, the art and the freedom as much as the desire
  • understand that "no" and silence mean the same thing
  • ask before you touch — every time, everyone
  • respect that some dance, some get inked, some have sex — all equally valid
  • are here to be present, not to collect anyone

It's not for you if you

  • are looking for a swingers' club or "free" sex
  • think a no is up for negotiation
  • film, photograph or "just check your phone" on the floor
  • see other people's bodies as something you're owed
  • can't be bothered to read the rest of this page
02

Safer space

By entering, you agree to Module's shared guidelines — everyone here is entitled to a space free from harassment, discrimination and exclusion. All genders, skin tones, sexual orientations, ages and body types are welcome. Consent, turned all the way up.

01 — THE GROUND RULE
Only an enthusiastic, verbal YES is a yes.
No means no. Silence means no. Mumbling means no. Uncomfortable body language means no. Anything that isn't a clear yes is a no.
02
No contact without clear consent.
Ask, every time, everyone. No exceptions, no matter how far the night has come.
03
Zero tolerance.
Racism, sexism, homophobia and transphobia will not be tolerated. Cross the line and you're out — and you stay out.
04
No staring or intrusive behaviour.
Looking is fine — respect boundaries. Give people the room to be here on their own terms.
05
No photos or video inside.
Phones away; your camera is covered at the door. It protects everyone. What happens on the floor stays on the floor.
06
The awareness team is here for you.
Feel uncomfortable, or see a boundary crossed? Reach out immediately — they're easy to find. We'll believe you, and we'll help.

About the ink

Ink is set at the studio.
Sober. Clean hands.

On the night, ink is an experience and a choice: flash on the wall, designs, placement, booking. You find the piece while the music runs.

The tattoo itself we set at Ink & Art's studio — sober, in clean hands, where it belongs. Kinky Sundays is where the piece is born. Larsbjørnsstræde is where it becomes skin.
